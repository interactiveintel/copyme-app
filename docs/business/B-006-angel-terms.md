# €1M angel — what we offer (B-006)

We present **two side-by-side options** so investors can pick their
preference. SAFE is faster, priced is cleaner — both result in the same
~10% effective dilution at our target.

## Option A — SAFE

* **Type:** Post-money SAFE (Y Combinator template)
* **Cap:** €10,000,000
* **Discount:** 20%
* **MFN:** Yes (Most-Favored-Nation if we issue better terms before the
  next priced round)
* **Conversion:** at next priced round of ≥ €1M

Pros:
* No board seat, no protective provisions until conversion
* Quick to sign — counsel can turn around in days
* Deferred valuation negotiation

Cons:
* Compounding dilution if other SAFEs stack
* Less "real" to some EU investors

## Option B — Priced Seed (alternative)

* **Pre-money:** €8,000,000
* **Round size:** €1,000,000
* **Post-money:** €9,000,000
* **Founder dilution:** 11.1%
* **Pro-rata:** Standard (not super-pro-rata)
* **Board:** 2 founders + 1 investor observer (no full seat)
* **Liquidation pref:** 1× non-participating
* **Anti-dilution:** Broad-based weighted average

Pros:
* Cleaner cap table, easier later rounds
* Real shareholder relationship; investor on the captable today

Cons:
* Slower (3-4 weeks vs 1 week for SAFE)
* Triggers preferred-stock + protective provisions

## Comparison at €15M Series A

| Metric | SAFE @ €10M cap | Priced @ €8M pre |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Founder dilution from this round | ~6.7% | 11.1% |
| Founder dilution after Series A (assume €5M @ €25M post) | ~26% | ~28% |
| Time to close | 1 week | 3-4 weeks |
| Investor IRR if exit at €100M | ~9× | ~10× |

We recommend **defaulting to SAFE** but accepting either at the
investor's preference.
