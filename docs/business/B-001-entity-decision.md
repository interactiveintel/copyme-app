# Legal home decision (B-001)

**Status:** recommendation drafted — pending sign-off
**Recommended:** **Delaware C-corp** (parent) + **Slovenia d.o.o.** (operating sub)
**Decision owners:** Paul Pereira · Jože Kralj

## Why two-entity

| Need | Why two-co fixes it |
| --- | --- |
| US fundraising (€1M angel, future Series A) | Investors expect Delaware C-corp |
| EU operations (Pimdom team, SI invoicing) | Need EU presence for VAT-MOSS, EU contracts |
| US payroll for Paul + future US hires | Delaware enables clean payroll/equity stacks |
| EU payroll for Jože + Pimdom team | SI d.o.o. handles EU labor + tax |
| Future ESOP | Delaware ISOs + EU equivalent under SI law |

## Structure

```
                ┌────────────────────────┐
                │  CopyMe, Inc. (Delaware) │  ← parent
                └────────────┬───────────┘
                             │  100% owned
                             ▼
                ┌────────────────────────┐
                │ CopyMe d.o.o. (Slovenia) │  ← operating sub
                └────────────────────────┘
```

* IP assigned to the Delaware parent (B-004).
* SI sub does an arm's-length services agreement with parent (cost-plus 7%).
* Bank accounts: Mercury (US) + NLB (SI).

## Alternatives considered

* **Single SI d.o.o.** — simpler legal stack but unappealing to US VCs and
  the equity / option pool is more painful.
* **Single Delaware C-corp with EU contractor** — cheap day-1 but blocks
  EU hiring / leases / consumer marketing.
