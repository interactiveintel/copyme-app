# Closing the round (B-012)

**Status:** runbook — to be executed once a lead confirms.

## Sequence

1. **Lead investor signs** — SAFE or priced (B-006). Other angels can
   ride the same instrument.
2. **Counsel issues docs** — share certificates (priced) or SAFE
   countersigned PDFs (SAFE).
3. **Wire instructions sent** — Mercury (US) for Delaware parent; NLB (SI)
   only used for SI sub salary float.
4. **Funds in NewCo bank** — confirm receipt. Document on cap table
   alongside investor name + amount.
5. **Cap table update** — `docs/business/B-002-cap-table.md` updated; new
   shareholders added to data room ACL.
6. **Founder + investor announcement (embargoed)** until Phase 1 LAUNCH
   (S-194); then social + PR push (S-193 + S-255).

## Embargo policy

Some investors will want to publicize day-1; we hold all financing
announcements until **48h after S-194 launch**. Rationale: the product
and the cohort numbers should drive the headline; the round is the
subhead. This avoids "vapor-funded" framing and aligns to the launch
press cycle.
